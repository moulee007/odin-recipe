chicken

Description

Indulge in the irresistible crunch of homemade crispy fried chicken.
Marinated in buttermilk and coated in a flavorful blend of spices, each succulent piece is fried to golden perfection.
The result is a mouthwatering dish with a crispy exterior that gives way to tender,
juicy chicken inside. Serve with your preferred sides for a classic comfort food experience that's sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds (about 1 kg) of chicken pieces (mix of drumsticks, thighs, and wings)
  • 2 cups (480 ml) buttermilk
  • 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust according to spice preference)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken:

    In a large bowl, pour the buttermilk over the chicken pieces, ensuring they are fully submerged.
    Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or preferably overnight. This helps tenderize the chicken and infuses it with flavor.

  2. Prepare the Breading Mixture:

    In a shallow dish, mix the flour, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Adjust the seasoning to your liking.

  3. Coat the Chicken:

    Take each piece of chicken out of the buttermilk, allowing any excess to drip off.

    Roll the chicken pieces in the seasoned flour mixture, ensuring they are fully coated. You can press the flour onto the chicken to make sure it sticks well.

  4. Rest the Coated Chicken:

    Place the coated chicken on a wire rack to allow excess flour to fall off and let the coating adhere to the chicken for a few minutes. This helps the breading stick better during frying.

  5. Heat the Oil:

    In a large, deep skillet or Dutch oven, heat about 2 inches (5 cm) of vegetable oil to 350째F (175째C). Use a kitchen thermometer to monitor the temperature.

  6. Fry the Chicken:

    Carefully place a few pieces of chicken into the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry each piece for about 12-15 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C), and the coating is golden brown and crispy.

    Use a slotted spoon or tongs to remove the fried chicken and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to absorb excess oil.

  7. Serve:

    Allow the chicken to rest for a few minutes before serving. Serve your crispy fried chicken with your favorite dipping sauces, coleslaw, or potato wedges.

Enjoy your homemade crispy fried chicken!
